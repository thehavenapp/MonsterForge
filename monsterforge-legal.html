A guide for parents and guardians · June 2025

👨‍👩‍👧 We built MonsterForge to be safe, fun, and transparent. This page answers the most common questions parents have about the app, spending, AI, and data.

Is MonsterForge Safe for Kids?

MonsterForge is designed to be kid-friendly and appropriate for all ages. The AI monster creation tool has built-in filters to prevent inappropriate content. However, we always recommend parents play alongside younger children and stay involved in their gaming experience.

Does MonsterForge Collect My Child's Data?

No. MonsterForge does not require account creation, does not collect a child's name, email, or personal details, and does not track location. The only data sent externally is the monster prompt text (e.g. "a fire-breathing hamster") which is sent to the Anthropic AI to generate the monster. No personal information is attached to this request. See our full Privacy Policy for details.

What Can Kids Spend Real Money On?

MonsterForge offers optional purchases:

  • Monster Eggs — $0.99 to $9.99 — hatch random monsters
  • Battle Pass — $4.99/month — extra features and monsters

All purchases are processed through Stripe, a secure payment processor. We strongly recommend enabling purchase restrictions or parental controls on your child's device to prevent unintended charges.

Virtual coins earned or purchased in-game have no real-world value and cannot be converted to money.

Is There Chat or Social Interaction?

The current version of MonsterForge does not include live chat, messaging, or direct social interaction between players. Battle features are simulated against AI opponents. There is no way for strangers to contact your child through MonsterForge.

How Do I Request Data Deletion?

Since we collect minimal data and do not require accounts, most user data is stored locally in the browser. To fully remove any data we may hold, email us at havenhelper1@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and we will process it within 30 days.

COPPA Compliance

MonsterForge complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child's information has been collected without consent, contact us immediately at havenhelper1@gmail.com.
